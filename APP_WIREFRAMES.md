## Key UX Principles

1. **Speed**: Show data fast, cache aggressively
2. **Clarity**: Clear visual hierarchy, scannable info
3. **Actionable**: Every screen has a clear purpose
4. **Insightful**: Don't just show stats, provide intelligence
5. **Beautiful**: Cricket-themed, professional design
